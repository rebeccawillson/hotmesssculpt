Class Formats

Heated Sculpt

A full-body sculpting class using infrared heat to maximize calorie burn and tone muscles in a supportive, energizing environment.

Heated Power Yoga

Dynamic yoga flows in a heated room designed to build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness for all levels.

Heated HIIT

High-Intensity Interval Training powered by heat and music to boost endurance, metabolism, and energy in quick, effective bursts.

Heated Mat Pilates

Core-focused Pilates exercises done on the mat in a heated environment to strengthen, lengthen, and stabilize your muscles.

Heated Strength

Strength training with weights and resistance in a heated room designed to increase muscle tone and power.
